About Us

Milev's Designs™ can offer the following services:

  • development of a website according to the customer’s choice;
  • development of personal blogs according to the customer’s choice;
  • websites with individual design;
  • dynamic and static websites;
  • technical support for websites that we’ve already developed;
  • mobile versions of websites for an easier access from mobile devices and tablets;
  • development of screen readers for the corresponding websites, specially developed for people with disabilities;
  • an opportunity for printing the website’s content on paper through the use of a printer.

Milev's Designs™ is an IT company that deals with website development and support. It was founded in August, 2010 in the city of Sofia, Bulgaria. Our team consists of: Viktor Milev (CEO; chief web designer), Nataliya Yasenova ('Websites Development'), Ivan Rosenov ('IT support'), Svetlana Kalinova ('Mobile websites and applications'), Ivana Trifonova ('Development of screen readers'), Yasen Nedelchev (Customer Relationship Manager), etc. We have more than 30 IT specialists who will develop a website (or any other IT service) based on your ideas. If you have difficulty with choosing, our specialists will be always ready to offer you professional ideas for your convenience.

We offer website developments based on the customer’s preferences. We help both start-ups and big successful companies by developing a website that will fit with their demands and preferences. Except for developing websites, we offer technical support as well. And we also offer an opportunity for different versions for websites – mobile versions and screen readers.
